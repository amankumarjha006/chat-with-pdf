# Chat with PDF 📄💬

Welcome to **Chat with PDF**! This application allows you to upload any PDF document—whether it's a textbook, a legal contract, a research paper, or a manual—and instantly ask questions about its content using advanced Artificial Intelligence.

Instead of reading through hundreds of pages, you can simply ask what you need to know, and the AI will find the answer for you, complete with exact page references so you can verify the information yourself.

---

## ✨ What can you do with it?

- **Instant Answers:** Ask complex questions about the document and get clear, concise answers in seconds.
- **Source Verification:** Every answer the AI gives includes the exact page numbers it used to find the information. No more guessing if the AI is making things up!
- **Smart Summaries & Suggestions:** As soon as you upload a document, the app will analyze it and suggest relevant questions you might want to ask.
- **Fast and Reliable:** Once a document is uploaded, it's securely cached. If you close the app and come back later, you won't have to wait for it to process again.
- **Works on Any Device:** Whether you are on your laptop or your phone, the app is fully responsive. On mobile, you get a clean, app-like experience with a slide-out menu.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use It

1. **Upload your Document:** 
   Drag and drop your PDF file into the upload area, or click to browse your files.
2. **Wait for Processing:** 
   The app will briefly analyze and index your document. You'll see a success message when it's ready.
3. **Start Chatting:** 
   Type your question in the chat box at the bottom. 
   - *Example:* "What is the main conclusion of this report?"
   - *Example:* "Summarize the section on financial risks."
4. **Use Suggestions:** 
   Not sure what to ask? Click on any of the suggested questions in the sidebar (or in the mobile menu) to instantly ask them. You can also click the refresh button next to "Suggested" to get new ideas.
5. **Check the Sources:** 
   Look at the small page number badges underneath the AI's response to see exactly where in the document the answer came from.

---

## 🌙 Customizing your Experience

- **Dark Mode:** Click the sun/moon icon in the top right corner to toggle between Light Mode and a sleek, easy-on-the-eyes Dark Mode.
- **Mobile Menu:** If you are using a phone, tap the hamburger menu (☰) in the top left to open the sidebar, where you can see document details, suggested questions, and an option to upload a new PDF.

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Data

- **Grounded Answers only:** The AI is strictly instructed to answer *only* based on the document you provided.
- **Local Caching:** Your document is temporarily processed into chunks to enable searching, optimizing speed without keeping permanent databases unless configured otherwise.
